About Me

Introduction

Hello! I am Mengji Zhang, a Postdoc Associate at Yale University. My research focuses on computational biology. If you are interested in collaboration with me, please contact mengji.zhang0809@gmail.com.

Background

I received my PhD in Biomedical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My work involves computational omics and molecular structure decoding. Now I am working at decoding the interaction between behaviors and neural activities in the brain with deep learning.

Interests

My interests include playing Nintendo games and watching Japanese animations.
